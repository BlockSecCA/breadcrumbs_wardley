# Cup of Tea

The final product delivered to customers - a prepared cup of tea.

## Product Description
- Hot brewed tea served in a cup
- Combines tea leaves, hot water, and serving vessel
- Ready-to-consume beverage
- Customer-facing end product

## Value Proposition
- Convenience - prepared and ready to drink
- Quality - properly brewed tea
- Experience - service and presentation
- Consistency - standardized preparation

## Strategic Position
This is the core product that customers actually purchase. All other components in the value chain exist to enable the delivery of this product.

## Quality Factors
- Tea brewing temperature and time
- Tea leaf quality and freshness
- Water quality
- Cup cleanliness and presentation
- Service speed and consistency
