# Electric Kettle

Modern electric kettle representing the evolution of water heating technology.

## Evolution from Traditional Kettle
- **Automation**: Automatic temperature control and shut-off
- **Precision**: Digital temperature settings for different tea types
- **Efficiency**: Faster heating, better energy efficiency
- **Safety**: Built-in safety features and overheating protection
- **Convenience**: One-button operation, programmable settings

## Advanced Features
- Temperature presets (160°F, 175°F, 185°F, 200°F, 212°F)
- Keep-warm function
- Digital display
- Variable temperature control
- Fast boiling capability
- Automatic shut-off

## Strategic Advantages
- **Consistency**: Eliminates human error in temperature control
- **Speed**: Faster service during peak times
- **Quality**: Optimal brewing temperatures for different teas
- **Efficiency**: Lower energy costs, reduced waste

## Investment Considerations
- Higher upfront cost vs. traditional kettle
- Energy savings over time
- Improved customer experience
- Staff training simplified
- Maintenance requirements

## Market Position
Electric kettles represent the current best practice for tea shops. The technology has standardized around digital temperature control and safety features.
