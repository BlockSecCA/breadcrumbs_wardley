# Tea

Tea leaves or tea bags used to brew the beverage.

## Product Types
- Black tea (Earl Grey, English Breakfast)
- Green tea (Sencha, Jasmine)
- Herbal tea (Chamomile, Peppermint)
- Specialty blends

## Quality Factors
- Leaf grade and origin
- Freshness and storage
- Flavor profiles
- Consistency batch to batch

## Supply Chain
- Tea suppliers and importers
- Quality certifications (organic, fair trade)
- Seasonal availability variations
- Price fluctuations

## Strategic Considerations
- Product differentiation opportunity
- Customer preference diversity
- Inventory management complexity
- Quality directly impacts customer satisfaction

## Cost Structure
- Raw material costs
- Storage and handling
- Waste from spoilage
- Variety vs. simplicity trade-offs
