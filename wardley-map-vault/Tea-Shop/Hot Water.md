# Hot Water

Heated water at the proper temperature for brewing tea.

## Specifications
- Temperature: 160-212°F depending on tea type
- Volume: Sufficient for daily service demand
- Quality: Clean, filtered water
- Timing: Available when needed

## Brewing Requirements
- **Green tea**: 160-180°F (delicate leaves)
- **Black tea**: 200-212°F (full extraction)
- **Herbal tea**: 212°F (boiling water)
- **Oolong tea**: 185-205°F (varies by type)

## Operational Process
1. Fill kettle with clean water
2. Heat to appropriate temperature
3. Maintain temperature during service
4. Monitor water quality and taste

## Quality Control
- Water temperature monitoring
- Regular equipment cleaning
- Water filtration maintenance
- Taste testing protocols

## Strategic Notes
Critical process capability that directly affects tea quality. Proper temperature control is essential for optimal flavor extraction and customer satisfaction.
