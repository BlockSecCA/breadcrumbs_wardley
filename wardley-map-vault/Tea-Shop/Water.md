# Water

Clean water supply for tea brewing.

## Quality Requirements
- Filtered or purified water
- Low mineral content (soft water preferred)
- No chlorine taste or odor
- Consistent quality

## Sources
- **Municipal water supply**: Requires filtration
- **Bottled water**: Higher cost, consistent quality
- **Water filtration system**: Investment in equipment
- **Well water**: Variable quality, testing required

## Quality Impact
- **Hard water**: Can create bitter flavors, mineral buildup
- **Chlorinated water**: Affects tea taste significantly  
- **Poor quality**: Ruins even premium tea
- **Optimal water**: Enhances tea flavor and aroma

## Strategic Considerations
- Cost vs. quality trade-offs
- Equipment investment decisions
- Regional water quality variations
- Customer taste expectations

## Operational Notes
Water quality is often overlooked but critically important for tea brewing. Many tea shops underestimate the impact of water quality on their product.
