# Business

The fundamental business need - providing tea service to customers who want a hot beverage.

## Customer Need
- Customers want a convenient, hot, refreshing drink
- Willing to pay for the service and experience
- Expects consistent quality and availability

## Business Model
- Retail tea service
- Direct customer interaction
- Value delivery through prepared beverages

## Strategic Position
This represents the core user need that drives the entire value chain. Understanding and serving this need effectively is what makes the business viable.
