# Power

Electrical power supply for tea shop operations.

## Service Details
- **Provider**: Local electrical utility
- **Service level**: Commercial electrical service
- **Voltage**: 120V/240V standard
- **Capacity**: Sufficient for all equipment

## Usage Patterns
- **Peak demand**: Morning and afternoon rush
- **Base load**: Lighting, refrigeration, POS systems
- **Variable load**: Kettles, grinders, heating equipment
- **Backup needs**: Critical for continuous service

## Cost Structure
- Monthly service charges
- Kilowatt-hour usage rates
- Peak demand charges
- Power factor considerations

## Strategic Position
Commodity utility service - standardized, reliable, regulated pricing. Focus should be on efficiency rather than supply alternatives.

## Risk Management
- Backup power considerations
- Electrical safety compliance
- Load balancing for equipment
- Energy efficiency opportunities

## Future Evolution
Power infrastructure is evolving toward renewable sources and smart grid integration, but this doesn't significantly impact day-to-day tea shop operations.
