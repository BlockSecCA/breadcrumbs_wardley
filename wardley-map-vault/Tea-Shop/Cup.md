# Cup

Physical vessel for serving the tea to customers.

## Product Specifications
- Ceramic or disposable cups
- Appropriate size (8-12 oz typical)
- Heat-resistant material
- Clean and presentable

## Supply Chain
- Standard manufactured product
- Multiple suppliers available
- Commodity pricing
- Reliable availability

## Operational Considerations
- Washing and sanitization (ceramic)
- Inventory management
- Cost per unit
- Storage requirements
- Breakage replacement

## Strategic Position
Essential but standardized component. Focus should be on cost efficiency and reliable supply rather than differentiation.
